Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Privacy-First Commitment: Social Downloader is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Social Downloader does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Social media account credentials or login information
  • URLs or links you paste into the app
  • Downloaded content or media files
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or app behavior tracking
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

2. How the App Works

Social Downloader operates as follows:

  • Local Processing: All URL parsing and download management happens directly on your device
  • API Communication: When you request a download, the app sends the URL to our backend API to retrieve download links. The API does not log or store URLs
  • Direct Downloads: Media files are downloaded directly to your device's storage
  • No Cloud Storage: Downloaded files remain only on your device; we never store them on our servers
  • Ephemeral Sessions: All download sessions are temporary and no data persists after completion

3. Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Internet Access: To communicate with our backend API and download media files
  • Storage Access: To save downloaded files to your device
  • Network State: To monitor connectivity and optimize download quality (WiFi vs mobile data)
  • Notifications: To show download progress and completion alerts

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and never for data collection.

4. Third-Party Services

Social Downloader does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app connects only to:

  • Our Backend API: Hosted at socialdownloader-api.onrender.com for URL parsing only
  • Social Media Platforms: To retrieve publicly accessible content URLs

5. Data Security

While we do not collect data, we implement security best practices:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No user data is stored on our servers
  • Downloaded files are stored locally using Android's secure storage APIs

6. Children's Privacy

Social Downloader does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, the app is safe for all ages, subject to parental guidance regarding content.

7. Copyright and Content Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to download content. Social Downloader:

  • Does not remove watermarks or DRM protection
  • Only provides access to publicly available content
  • Does not support downloading private or restricted content
  • Respects platform terms of service

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over:

  • Files downloaded to your device (managed through your device's file manager)
  • App permissions (managed through your device settings)
  • App uninstallation (removes all app data from your device)

10. International Users

Social Downloader is available worldwide. Since we do not collect personal data, GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations are not applicable. The app operates the same way for all users regardless of location.
